CONTROLS & INSTRUCTIONS

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Each player selects a spider champion.
  • The spiders are placed on opposite ends of a string.
  • To move and attack, you must “fast click” the keys.
  • The goal is to push the opponent's spider off the string.
  • Victory is declared when one spider falls or is forced to retreat to its starting position.

GAME CONTROLS

Player 1: Use the 'W' key.

Player 2: Use the 'UP ARROW' key.
